Final model: linear-kernel SVC (C=1), selected after 5-fold stratified cross-validation across 9 classifiers. Trained on 1,000 significant earthquakes, 1995–2023 (USGS). This page runs the exact trained model client-side in JavaScript — verified to match the Python model's predictions on 1,000/1,000 test rows. Confidence bars are a softmax over decision margins (an approximation — the model wasn't trained with calibrated probabilities).
